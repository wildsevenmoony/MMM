[h1]Moony's Magnificent Modules[/h1]

[b]Moony's Magnificent Modules[/b], or [b]MMM[/b], is an Arma 3 utility mod for mission makers, Zeus operators, and unit scenarios.

It adds a collection of practical Eden and Zeus modules for faster mission setup, live Zeus control, medical staging, ambient scenes, AI handling, vehicle wrecks, and configurable gear randomization.

[b]Features[/b]
[list]
[*] Eden modules for mission setup
[*] Zeus modules with ZEN integration
[*] Apply configurable ACE medical damage to units
[*] Add base medic healing actions to synced objects
[*] Turn individual AI or whole groups stationary
[*] Apply ambient animations, switchMove, and playMove animations
[*] Preview supported animations in Eden
[*] Spawn vehicles as wrecks with optional ACE cookoff/explosion behavior
[*] Mark vehicles or objects as Mobile HQs with respawn/teleport support
[*] Randomize full gear or specific categories:
[list]
[*] Backpacks
[*] Goggles
[*] Headgear
[*] Uniforms
[*] Vests
[*] Weapons and attachments
[/list]
[*] Small Zeus utility modules for ammo, fuel, dragging/carrying, crater cleanup, and more
[*] Optional infection Zeus helpers when the matching Moony infection systems are loaded
[*] Custom medic insignia
[/list]

[b]Requirements[/b]
[list]
[*] CBA
[*] ACE
[*] ZEN
[*] Moony's Magnificent Base
[/list]

[b]Usage[/b]
In Eden, look for modules under [b]Moony's Magnificent Modules[/b]. Place a module, configure its attributes, and synchronize it to the relevant units, vehicles, or objects.

In Zeus, the ZEN modules provide quick live-mission tools for medical effects, AI behavior, object utilities, gear randomization, and other mission-control tasks.

[b]Notes[/b]
MMM was built for my own unit [url=https://discord.gg/Ju8FqJ3bpN]STAF[/url] and workflow, so some behavior is intentionally practical, specific, and occasionally a little rough around the edges. It should still be useful for other groups that want a compact toolbox of Eden and Zeus helpers.
